Lightrunner: The Shifting Path

In the Neon Dunes, a lone Runner carries a spark of fading light. The world has been dimming for ages, and only by crossing the shifting trails can the Runner restore balance.

Your choices shape the dune’s colors, sounds, and fate.

Instructions

• Use Arrow Keys to move left and right.
• Avoid obstacles.
• Reach the end of the track.
• Make decisions when prompted.
